Also note that these weights are per bag, so If you have multiple bag, indicate the specific weight of each bag multiplied by the number of bags.

Custom

Every item shipped has to pass through Customs. This means they have to be cleared before they are allowed passage into the country.

Truth is… Custom rates are expensive and they suck. Dealing with them will cost you a lot of money and time.

Don't fret though. We care about you and have decided to help you deal with Custom Duties upon arrival of your goods for a heavily subsidised price of ₦9600 per box.

This means we will go through the stress of getting your goods through customs directly to where you want it

This is optional though. You can decide not to take this offer and go through Customs yourself. It would only cost you time and so much money!
